Force of Will

Bio Music


The band Force of Will came together in 2025 as a project by Swedish bandleader Albin Sundstrom. Sundstrom, a bassist, upon arriving in America to study jazz performance at the Berklee College of Music, put together this band so he could bring them back to tour in Sweden. Force of Will comprises bassist Albin Sundstrom, drummer Gilberto Moretti-Hamilton, pianist Anthony Yuen, and trumpeter Forrest Aubrey. All members met in their freshmen year at Berklee, and in the following summer toured Sweden, playing at venues in Stockholm and Visby.


Force of Will received great applause for its tour in Sweden, many audience members even saying they were the best jazz musicians they had ever seen. There was great interest expressed by the audiences in the band returning for a second tour in Sweden.



Force of Will primarily plays jazz music, with their repertoire featuring original compositions by bandleader Sundstrom, classic jazz songs by artists such as Joe Henderson and Woody Shaw, and even compositions by modern artists such as Hiromi. The band loves to explore different ways of interpreting and playing old songs, and turning those songs into something that feels new.
